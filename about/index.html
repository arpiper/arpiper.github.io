About Me

A University of Washington grad with a degree in Applied and Computational Math Sciences. I love all things math, science and the outdoors.

My primary technologies are JavaScript, Python, PHP and HTML5 and CSS3. I have a preference for the lighter JavaScript frameworks like Svelte and VueJS and prefer using Python/Django for backend use due to its ease of readability. However this doesn't limit my openness to new or different technologies for any project.

Away from a computer I can be found rock climbing, running and generally spending time in the outdoors or hanging out with friends and playing board games.
